about cafe joyo

Café Joyo is a casual and fun indoor/outdoor pure veg café located between NH-79 and Ujjain–Jaora Road, serving wholesome dishes and drinks with a touch of local flair.

In a warm and relaxed environment, our professional chefs from different parts of the country craft authentic, delicious vegetarian food. Our logo reflects nature, the open road, and a joyful spirit — symbolizing your favorite place to enjoy good food on the go.
